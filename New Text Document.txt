SIMULATION: Create an alert policy for sharing of files externally.  Set to high severity.
Step 1: log in to Microsoft defender portal
Step 2: go to tab 'policies and ruels'
step 3: click on 'alert policy' --> new alert policy
step 4: name the policy, set severity as 'high' and set category as 'information governance' and click next
step 5: Set activity is 'Shared file externally' 
step 6: turn on radio button as 'every time an activity matches the rule'
step 7: click next and add Email recipient
step 8: review the settings 
step 9: done and the policy made.
